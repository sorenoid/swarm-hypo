Appendix A, Frequently Asked Questions

Please feel free to email questions to the author, Dan Cervelli.

How does auto-scaling work?
See Paragraph 2 of Wave Options of Section III.

What about bias on the helicorders?
Bias is removed on a per line basis from the helicorders.

What does red mean on the helicorders?
Red means that the helicorder is clipped according to the current settings. See Other Options, Section IV. It does not necessarily mean that the signal is clipped.
